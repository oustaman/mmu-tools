Academic Deadlines Tracker

This tool helps you keep track of important academic deadlines throughout the year.

Features

  • View all academic deadlines in chronological order
  • Pin important deadlines to keep them visible at the top
  • Filter by different categories (Teaching, Submission, Research, etc.)
  • See a countdown for each deadline
  • Search for specific deadlines
  • View your next upcoming deadline at a glance
  • See a 14-day calendar view with deadline indicators
Tips for Using This Tracker

This tracker is designed to help you manage important academic dates and deadlines.

  • Use the search box at the top to find specific deadlines
  • Click the pin icon next to any deadline to keep it at the top of the list
  • Filter deadlines by category using the buttons at the top
  • Deadlines are color-coded by their proximity: red for urgent (within 7 days), yellow for approaching (within 30 days), and blue for further away
  • Toggle between dark and light modes using the icon in the top-right corner
  • The "Next Upcoming Deadline" shows your most imminent deadline
  • The 14-day calendar gives you a visual overview of upcoming deadlines

"Clear Custom Deadlines" will delete only deadlines you've added. Default university deadlines will remain.
"Restore University Deadlines" will bring back any deleted university deadlines without affecting your custom deadlines.
