From MVP to Prototype

Your BPEDI MVP

  • ‚Ä¢ Focus: Minimal features to validate demand
  • ‚Ä¢ Question: "Will people pay for this?"
  • ‚Ä¢ Example: Landing page, pricing, basic feature list

Your UXCI Prototype

  • ‚Ä¢ Purpose: Test usability
  • ‚Ä¢ Focus: Minimal fidelity to validate user experience
