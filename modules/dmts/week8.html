Music: From Physical to Streaming

1999: Napster - File Sharing

Illegal but revolutionary. "All music should be free". Industry panic.

2001: iPod + iTunes

$0.99/song model. Legal downloads. 1000 songs in your pocket.

2008: Spotify - Streaming Era

Unlimited music, monthly subscription. Access over ownership. Algorithms curate playlists.

Present: Streaming Dominance

600M+ Spotify users. Physical sales <10%. Artists paid per stream, not per album.

From owning music to renting access. Industry restructured completely.
