Understanding Domestication Theory

Domestication: The process by which a new technology becomes familiar, routine, and integrated into everyday practices and social relationships

Four stages of domestication:

1. Appropriation

Acquiring the technology, bringing it into your life

2. Objectification

Displaying it, incorporating it into physical space

3. Incorporation

Using it in daily routines and practices

4. Conversion

Using it to express identity and connect with others

Mobile Phone Domestication in Action

Appropriation (1990s-2000s)

"I'm getting a mobile for emergencies" ‚Üí Everyone gets one

Objectification (2000s)

Phone cases, customization, displaying brand (iPhone vs Android)

Incorporation (2010s)

First thing you check in morning, last at night. Always within reach. Anxiety when battery low

Conversion (present)

Your phone IS your identity - contacts, photos, social media presence, digital wallet
