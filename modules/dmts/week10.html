Week 10: Turning Research into Arguments

Week 10 of 11

Your Report Structure

2,250 words with clear sections

Introduction (~300 words)

Technology chosen, why it matters, what you'll cover

Development History (~600 words)

From prototype to current form. Key milestones. Statistical growth data.

User Base & Adoption (~600 words)

Demographics. Who uses it? How did it become mainstream? Domestication process.

Social Transformation (~600 words)

How it changed society. Work, communication, identity, culture.

Conclusion (~150 words)

Summary of key points. Current state and future outlook.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Just describing without analyzing

‚Üí Need: WHY it mattered, HOW it changed society

‚ùå Statistics without context

"Smartphone users increased 500% between 2010-2020."

‚Üí Need: What does this mean? Why did it happen? Social implications?

‚ùå Vague claims

"Computers changed everything and made life better."

‚Üí Need: Specific changes, evidence, nuanced view (pros AND cons)

‚ùå Poor referencing

"According to a study..." or "Research shows..."

‚Üí Need: Actual citation with author, year, source
