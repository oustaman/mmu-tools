What is the Internet?

The Internet is a global network of interconnected computers

It's the infrastructure that enables digital technologies to communicate

Key distinction:

Internet

The physical network infrastructure - cables, servers, protocols

World Wide Web

One service that runs ON the internet - websites, browsers, hyperlinks

The Dark Side: Challenges & Concerns

Privacy & surveillance

Companies and governments tracking online behavior. Data breaches. Loss of anonymity.

Misinformation & polarization

Fake news spreads faster than truth. Echo chambers. Conspiracy theories.

Digital divide

Not everyone has equal access. Reinforces existing inequalities.

Mental health impacts

Social media anxiety. Cyberbullying. FOMO. Addiction.
