User Journey Maps

Shows the complete user experience over time:

  • ‚Ä¢ Stages: Awareness ‚Üí Consideration ‚Üí Usage ‚Üí Completion
  • ‚Ä¢ Touchpoints: Where they interact (app, website, physical space)
  • ‚Ä¢ Actions: What they do at each stage
  • ‚Ä¢ Emotions: How they feel (graph showing highs/lows)
  • ‚Ä¢ Pain Points: Where frustration occurs
  • ‚Ä¢ Opportunities: Where you can improve

Choosing Your 2 Artefacts

Ask yourself:

  • ‚Ä¢ Which artefacts best support YOUR specific project?
  • ‚Ä¢ Which show your research/design thinking most clearly?

Quality Over Quantity

What makes a GOOD artefact?

  • ‚úìClear: Easy to understand at a glance
  • ‚úìDetailed: Shows real thought and research
  • ‚úìPurposeful: Actually contributed to your design
  • ‚úìProfessional: Well-presented, not rushed
