How to Use the 5 Whys Technique

  1. 1. Start with the surface problem - what users are experiencing or complaining about
  2. 2. Ask "Why?" to dig into the cause - what's making this happen?
  3. 3. Keep asking "Why?" for each answer - usually 3-5 times
  4. 4. Stop when you reach the root cause - the fundamental issue that needs solving
  5. 5. Your design solution should address the root cause, not just the surface problem
