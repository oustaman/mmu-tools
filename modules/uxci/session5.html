Rapid ideation technique

Crazy 8s

8 sketches in 8 minutes

A technique to break past your first ‚Äî usually obvious ‚Äî idea.

1
Fold paper into 8 sections (or use 8 boxes)
2
Pick ONE screen (e.g., home screen)
3
Draw 8 different versions ‚Äî 1 minute each
4
No erasing ‚Äî just move to the next box

Goal: volume over perfection. Often your 7th or 8th idea is the best.

Making sketches readable

Annotation Matters

Your sketches need notes explaining interactions ‚Äî that's what shows your UX thinking.

‚ùå Weak annotation

  • ‚Üí Drawing with no labels at all
  • ‚Üí "Button" (what does it do?)
  • ‚Üí No indication of state changes

‚úÖ Strong annotation

  • ‚Üí "Search ‚Üí filters screen"
  • ‚Üí "Shows loading spinner while fetching"
  • ‚Üí "Disabled until form is valid"
  • ‚Üí "Confirms before deleting"

Annotations show you understand the UX ‚Äî not just the visuals.
