Crazy 8s Exercise

8 sketches in 8 minutes

A rapid ideation technique to overcome perfectionism

  • 1.Fold paper into 8 sections (or use 8 boxes)
  • 2.Pick ONE screen to sketch (e.g., home screen)
  • 3.Draw 8 different versions - 1 minute each
  • 4.No erasing! Just move to next box

Goal: Volume over perfection. Explore many ideas quickly.

Activity: Crazy 8s (10 min)

Annotation Matters

Your sketches need notes explaining interactions

‚ùå Bad Annotation:

  • ‚Ä¢ Just drawing with no labels
  • ‚Ä¢ "Button" (what does it do?)
  • ‚Ä¢ No indication of state changes

‚úÖ Good Annotation:

  • ‚Ä¢ "Search button ‚Üí filters screen"
  • ‚Ä¢ "Shows loading spinner while fetching"
  • ‚Ä¢ "Disabled until form valid"
  • ‚Ä¢ "Confirms before deleting"

Annotations show you understand the UX, not just the visuals
