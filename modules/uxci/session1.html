What is User Experience (UX)?

Simple definition:

How a person feels when interacting with a product, service, or system

  • ‚Ä¢ Not just about looks - it's about how it works
  • ‚Ä¢ Not just digital - applies to physical spaces, services, anything people interact with
  • ‚Ä¢ Not just one moment - it's the entire journey

Good UX = People can do what they need to do, easily, without thinking about it

Classic Bad UX Examples

üîê Password Requirements Hell

"Must be 8-12 characters, include uppercase, lowercase, number, special character, not used in last 5 passwords..." Then you forget it immediately.

üç™ Cookie Pop-ups

"We value your privacy" while showing 400 toggles to opt-out of tracking. Dark patterns at their finest.

üì± Unsubscribe Nightmares

üéØ Where's the Button?

What Makes These Bad?

  • ‚ùå They make users think too much - cognitive overload
  • ‚ùå They prioritize business over user needs - dark patterns
  • ‚ùå They hide important information - poor visibility
  • ‚ùå They don't give feedback - is it loading? Did it work?
  • ‚ùå They ignore accessibility - not everyone interacts the same way

Good UX does the opposite of all of these
