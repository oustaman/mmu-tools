Monitoring Process Overview

This document outlines the key stages and responsibilities for monitoring student engagement during Semester 1 of the 2025/6 academic year. The process is divided into two main phases: Early Engagement (Weeks 1-2) and Continued Monitoring (ongoing).

Phase Timeframe Trigger Key Actions
Early Engagement Weeks 1 & 2 0% attendance in both weeks Inclusion on withdrawal report, Academic Warning sent, 2-week response period
Continued Monitoring Ongoing throughout semester <50% attendance for 3 weeks Flag for Academic Warning, SPT review, Engagement Team support offered
