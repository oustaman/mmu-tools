About this list

Have you been confused in meetings? I was nonplussed (very confused) when I MTUK (move to the UK) and didn't have this collection.

As a foreigner who a) reads local news. b) works with British Universities and c) teaching young people, I ended up hoarding up a massive collection of things that make no sense. At least now they do.

All these things are stuff I've picked up over the years, simply by living in the UK.
